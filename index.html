MapSH: Open-defecation mapping and mobile verification tool

These are open-defecation free sites from the Indian Ministry of Drinking Water & Sanitation. They can be verified through a mobile crowdsourcing tool built on top of mSewage.

Sarah Fathallah, Thomas Levine, Juan C. Müller and Daniel Shemie made this at the Toilet Hackers Sanitation Hackathon on December 2, 2012.

Contact us at mapshit@googlegroups.com.
