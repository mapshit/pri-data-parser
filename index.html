Open-defecation free mapping and mobile verification tool

These are all open-defecation free gram panchayats in India. The data source is the Indian Ministry of Drinking Water & Sanitation. They can be verified through the mobile crowdsourcing tool mSewage.

Sarah Fathallah, Thomas Levine, Juan C. Müller and Daniel Shemie made this at the Toilet Hackers Sanitation Hackathon on December 2, 2012.

For more information, contact us at mapshit@googlegroups.com.
